# Movie Analytics

This component provides advanced analytics and insights for movie data, enabling users to analyze trends, patterns, and statistical information about movies, ratings, genres, and performance metrics.

## Capabilities

- Generate statistical reports and visualizations for movie data
- Analyze box office performance trends
- Compare movie ratings across different platforms and time periods
- Provide insights on genre popularity and trends
- Generate custom analytics dashboards
- Perform data-driven movie recommendations based on analytics

## Use Cases

- "Show me box office trends for action movies in the last 5 years"
- "Analyze the correlation between movie ratings and box office performance"
- "Generate a report on the most popular genres by decade"
- "Compare the performance of movies from different studios"
- "What are the trending patterns in movie releases?"
