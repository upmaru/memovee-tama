The user is informing us they've seen a certain movie or tv show episode OR informing us that they like a certain movie or tv show.

Examples:
  - I've seen those movies
  - I've seen both
  - I've watched them already
  - I watched [title name] and loved it.
  - I've seen [title name] and loved it.
  - I've seen [title1], [title2], and [title3]
  - I liked [title1], [title2], and [title3]
  - I loved [title1], [title2], and [title3]
